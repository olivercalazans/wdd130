Our Story

It started as a simple friends company. What began with a couple of old rafts and a shared love for whitewater has grown into a full‑service adventure provider. Over the years, we’ve guided thousands of visitors through the most thrilling rapids, always prioritizing safety, fun, and respect for nature. Our story is built on the passion of our guides and the smiles of our adventurers.
